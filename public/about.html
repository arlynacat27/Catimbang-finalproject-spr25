Why This App Exists

This app was created to help people take small steps toward better mental health. By tracking your mood, writing down your thoughts, and receiving uplifting quotes and advice, you can start to recognize emotional patterns and practice mindfulness every day.

Benefits of Mental Health Journaling

  • Gain insight into your emotional patterns
  • Reduce stress and anxiety through reflection
  • Develop a daily mindfulness habit
  • Stay motivated with quotes and prompts

How to Use This App

  1. Visit the Home page to check in with your mood and write a journal entry.
  2. Read your daily motivational quote and piece of advice.
  3. Visit the Dashboard to review past entries and see trends.
